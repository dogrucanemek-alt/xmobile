## Full Description (EN)

**Your personal AI fashion assistant.**

xmobile eliminates the morning stress of choosing what to wear. It analyzes your wardrobe, reads the weather, and recommends the most suitable outfits based on your body measurements and skin tone.

**What you can do:**

👗 **Smart Outfit Suggestions**
AI learns your wardrobe and suggests daily outfits based on weather, occasion, and your personal style.

📸 **Try Before You Buy**
Photograph clothing you like in a store. AI instantly checks compatibility with your wardrobe and lets you search on Trendyol.

👗 **Virtual Try-On**
Digitally try on clothes to see how they'll look on you before buying.

📊 **Weekly Style Report**
Your most-worn items, category breakdown, and AI's personal style analysis.

🌍 **Community - Discover**
Discover outfits from other users, like, and get inspired.

🔔 **Morning Notification**
Get a daily outfit suggestion at the time you choose every morning.

**Pro Features:**
- Unlimited outfit suggestions
- Virtual Try-On
- 3D Avatar
- Advanced AI analysis
